Old is Cool Records

Website Development Plan

Website Name

"Old is Cool Records" is a name that makes it clear what the subject or material of the site, which is old records, which will attract lovers of such products.

Website Purpose

The site provides a registration form so members can buy, advertise, trade and sell old records at their leisure.

User Scenarios

  1. How do I purchase this specific disc?
  2. How do I contact the seller directly and negotiate a discount?
  3. How can I register my own albums that I want to sell?
  4. What is considered an old album that fits the site's standards?

Color Usage:

  • Black (Primary Cool): Used for headers, primary buttons, and main navigation. Creates a trustworthy and professional feel.
  • Dark cyan - lime green (Secondary Cool): Used for success messages, positive actions, and secondary buttons. Represents growth and freshness.
  • Scarlet (Accent Warm): Used for important alerts, sale indicators, and call-to-action elements. Draws attention to key areas.

Typography

Primary Font (Caveat): Basically used in the website header, bringing a less office-like atmosphere and more focused on entertainment, despite being a sales website, since the focus is on music between 1960 and 1980. It can also be used in special and non-standard conditions, such as flash discounts, etc.

Secondary Font (Nanum Gothic ): Used for body in general like headings, text and interface elements. Its excellent readability makes it perfect for product descriptions and detailed information.
